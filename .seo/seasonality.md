# DOKIPOKI Seasonality Calendar

Use this calendar when interpreting GSC reports.

## January-February

Focus:

- birthdays
- personažai
- animatoriai vaikams
- company/family winter events

Watch:

- early spring inquiries for darželio išleistuvės may begin.

## March-June

Focus:

- darželio išleistuvės
- kindergarten/group events
- spring birthdays

Interpretation:

- This is the main graduation season. Rising impressions/clicks for išleistuvės queries are expected.
- After June, drops for these queries are seasonal, not necessarily a ranking failure.

## July-August

Focus:

- Rugsėjo 1 / Mokslo ir žinių diena
- kindergarten bookings for school-year-start events
- summer company/family events
- birthdays

Action:

- Refresh school-year-start content before July if possible.
- Treat kindergartens as the primary and most frequent buyer for Rugsėjo 1 animator bookings.
- Strengthen internal links to relevant program pages.

Interpretation:

- July-August is the active booking window.
- September itself is mostly too late for SEO work, except last-minute demand and next-year learning.

## September-October

Focus:

- Helovynas
- early B2B Kalėdos / company Christmas events
- autumn birthdays
- company/family events

Action:

- Refresh Halloween content by early September.
- Refresh company Christmas event content immediately after summer.
- Start internal linking toward Christmas/company event pages in September.
- Add clear booking CTA before October demand peak.

## October-December

Focus:

- Kalėdos
- Kalėdų Senelis
- Elfai
- Grinčas / seasonal winter programs where appropriate
- company family events

Action:

- Consumer-facing Christmas content should be refreshed by October.
- B2B Christmas/company event content should already be live in September.
- Strengthen contact and reservation CTAs.

## Year-round Baseline

Always monitor:

- `/`
- `/personazai/`
- `/programos/`
- `/programos/gimtadieniai/`
- high-impression persona pages
- company event page

Stable query groups:

- animatoriai vaikams
- animatoriai Vilniuje
- personažai vaikų gimtadieniui
- vaikų gimtadienio programa
- personažai vaikams
